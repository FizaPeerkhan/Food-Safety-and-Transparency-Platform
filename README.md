# Food-Safety-and-Transparency-Platform
This project is a Food Safety and Ingredient Transparency Platform that helps users understand the health impact of packaged food products. The system analyzes ingredient lists of food products and highlights potentially harmful ingredients, allergens, and disease-specific risks.

Users can search for a product and receive a detailed report showing ingredient risk levels, allergens present, and health warnings for specific conditions such as diabetes, heart disease, or gluten intolerance.

The platform aims to improve consumer awareness, food transparency, and safety in packaged foods.
